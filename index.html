Frequently Asked Questions

1. Open the app and grant microphone permissions when prompted

2. Select your source language from the language picker

3. Choose your target language for translation

4. Tap the microphone button and start speaking

5. Your speech will be transcribed in real-time and translated automatically

After recording, tap the "Save" button to store your transcription and translation as a note. You can organize notes by categories like Personal, Work, or Study.

Speak2Text supports 20+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Arabic, Russian, Hindi, and many more.

Our app uses Yandex Translate API for high-quality translations. Accuracy varies by language pair, but generally provides excellent results for most supported languages.

Speech recognition requires an internet connection, but you can view and manage your saved notes offline. For best results, ensure you have a stable internet connection.

Access your notes from the Notes tab. You can search, filter by category, edit, and share your saved transcriptions and translations.

Troubleshooting

Microphone not working

  • Check that you've granted microphone permissions in Settings
  • Ensure your device's microphone is not being used by another app
  • Try restarting the app

Translation not appearing

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify that you've selected both source and target languages
  • Try speaking more clearly and at a normal pace

App crashes or freezes

  • Close the app completely and restart it
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device if the problem persists
